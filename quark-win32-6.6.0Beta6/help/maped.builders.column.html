 2.4.5. Columns (and Pipes)

Columns are basically more of the same, after arches and bevels, tho there are differences due to the shape and likely uses. For the moment there are only patch-based columns ('prisms' from the prism builder have similar functionality for brushes).

 Making columns

 tiglari - 03 Mar 2001   [ Top ] 

There's only one menu item for columns & pipes, Column. As usual, resize the block to change the shape of the column or pipe. And there are lots of specifics to experiment with. For example you can make a round porthole to fit into a square aperture by checking inverse and unchecking open.

One difference between pipe/column and the other shapebuilders at the moment is that for a pipe with thickness, the outside texture comes off the wall labeled `back', the inside off the one labelled `front', so they can be different.

Unfortunately there isn't yet any facility for making pipes that are bent, we need some kind of replication of the functionality of q3radiant's `patch bend mode', but don't have it yet.
