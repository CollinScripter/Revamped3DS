 2.4. Shape Builders

  [ Prev - Up - Next ] 

Shape-builders are a method for building complex objects, such as arches, stairways and more, on the basis of a boxy, 6-faced brush that gives the general dimensions of the object, plus additional specifics for details.

The most useful shape-builders are probably the ones for patch based arches (for Q3-engine games) and brush-based arches (earlier games), so these are discussed first, and most extensively.
